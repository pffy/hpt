Function list

Use HanyuPinyinTools custom functions to convert Chinese text to Hanyu Pinyin.

If the text in cell A1 is 中国, then you may use any of the following custom functions:

  • =HANYUPINYIN(A1)
    Converts text or range of text to Hanyu Pinyin with tone numbers.
    RESULT: zhong1 guo2
  • =HANYUPINYIN_TONELESS(A1)
    Converts text or range of text to Hanyu Pinyin with no tones.
    RESULT: zhong guo
  • =HANYUPINYIN_TONEMARKS(A1)
    Converts text or range of text to Hanyu Pinyin with backwards-compatible tone marks.
    RESULT: zhōng guó

    TONE 3 EXAMPLE: 好得很
    RESULT: hăo de hĕn
  • =HANYUPINYIN_TONEMARKS_ISO(A1)
    Converts text or range of text to Hanyu Pinyin with ISO 7098 compliant tone marks.
    RESULT: zhōng guó

    TONE 3 ISO EXAMPLE: 好得很
    ISO RESULT: hǎo de hěn
